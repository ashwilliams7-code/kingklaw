What You Actually Get

Imagine hiring a super-smart team member who works 24/7, never takes a day off, and gets better every month. That's what King Klaw gives your business.

🤖 Your Own AI Employee

You get your own AI agent that works autonomously — meaning it thinks, acts, and handles tasks on its own without you telling it what to do every time. It learns your business, remembers your clients, and makes decisions just like a real employee would.

📧 It Handles Your Emails

Client sends you an email at 11pm? Your AI reads it, understands what they need, drafts a professional response, and sends it — while you sleep. No more waking up to 47 unread emails.

💬 It Sends SMS Follow-Ups

Your AI automatically sends SMS reminders, follow-ups, and updates to your clients. Appointment reminders, document requests, check-ins — all sent at the perfect time, without you lifting a finger.

📞 It Answers & Makes Calls

Inbound calls get answered professionally 24/7. Your AI can also make outbound calls — following up with leads, confirming appointments, and checking in with clients. It sounds natural and knows your business inside out.

📊 It Manages Your CRM

Every call, email, and SMS is automatically logged in your CRM. Client details, conversation history, next steps — all tracked without any data entry from you. You always know exactly where every client is in your pipeline.

📅 It Books Your Appointments

Clients can book meetings through any channel — phone, SMS, email. Your AI checks your calendar, finds the right time, sends confirmations, and even sends reminders the day before. No more back-and-forth scheduling.

🧠 It Remembers Everything

Your AI remembers every client, every conversation, every preference. When John calls back 3 months later, your AI knows who he is, what you discussed, and picks up right where you left off. Your clients feel like they're your only client.

⚡ It Works 24/7/365

Weekends, public holidays, 3am — your AI never stops. While your competitors' phones go to voicemail, your business is always on. That means more leads captured, more clients served, and more revenue — without hiring more staff.

You're hiring a full-time employee who costs less than $30/day, never calls in sick, works every hour of every day, handles emails + SMS + calls + CRM + scheduling simultaneously, and gets smarter every single month.
